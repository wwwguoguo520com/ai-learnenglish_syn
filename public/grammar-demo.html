📖 示例句子1：定语从句
The book {that} I read [yesterday] was very interesting.

原句: The book that I read yesterday was very interesting.

📖 示例句子2：状语从句
I will call you {when} I arrive [home].

原句: I will call you when I arrive home.

📖 示例句子3：复合句（含定语）
(The very good) student {who} studies [hard] will succeed.

原句: The very good student who studies hard will succeed.

📖 示例句子4：并列句（含连词）
I like apples and she likes oranges.

原句: I like apples and she likes oranges.

📚 使用说明
  • 手动标注: 选中文本 → 选择语法类别 → 保存
  • AI自动分析: 配置API → 点击"🤖 AI分析" → 自动标注
  • 符号说明:
    • ( ) 圆括号 - 定语（修饰名词）
    • [ ] 方括号 - 状语（修饰动词/形容词）
    • { } 花括号 - 从句引导词（that, which, who等）
